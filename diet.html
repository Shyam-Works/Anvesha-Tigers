Diet of Tigers

Tigers are powerul creatures with a large appetite. Tiger have different strategies to hunt dow prey. From occasionally munching grass to killing and eating full grown bears, they are extremely adaptable. I will show you how powerful tigers are in one picture.

Strategies of Hunting

Stalking

Using the cover of vegetation, tigers patiently stalk their prey. The stripes help them to blend in with their background.

Ambushing

When tigers are close enough to their prey, they suddenly lunge. Tigers like to go for the back, with the element of suprise. With one quick bite on the neck, tigers use their razor-sharp teeth and claws to finish their prey.

Swim Power

Unlike many big cats, cat are suprisingly good at swimming. There was actually a tiger who swam for 120 km! When a water chase happens, tigers strong swimming give them an advantage.

Nocturnal Vision

Tigers have excellent night vision. This helps them to hunt in the night. Especially since most of tigers' prey have poor night sight.

Patience

Tigers have a lot of patience. They can wait for hours, waiting for the perfect time to strike.

Main Prey

Large Prey

Large Deer

Tigers like to hunt sambar deer, chital, hog deer, and barasingha.

Wild boar

Tigers love to prey on wild boar which are plentiful in numbers.

Buffalo and gaur

Tigers sometimes hunt these large animals.

Medium-sized Prey

Small Deer

Tigers prey on antelope, mujac, and serow.

Wild Goats and Sheep

For mountainous tigers, they often hunt goats like tahr.

Small Prey

Hares and Rabbits

Sometimes tigers hunt them because larger prey is scarce.

Porcupines and Pangolins

These are hard to hunt, but sometimes tigers prey on them.

Other (Occasional) Prey

Birds

Sometimes tigers hunt pheasants and peafowl(peacocks).

Fish and Reptiles

Since tigers can swim, they can hunt fish and caimans. They also rarely hunt snakes.

More Rare Prey that Tiger Eat

Cows and Buffalo

Tigers only turn to livestock when other prey is scarce.

Goats and Sheep

Just like buffalo, tigers rarely hunt them.

Carrion

Tigers will sometimes scavenge from other predators' kills.

Primates

Tigers will rarely prey on monkeys.

Rodents

Rodents are a last-resort prey.
