About Tigers

This website will talk about tigers. Tigers are majestic creatures that are part of the Felidae family. Tigers are in the same family as lions, cheetahs, jaguars, leopards, and even house cats. Tigers have a large body with unique stripes. They have a large diet, and they can live pretty long lives. I won't reveal too much information, as this website will tell the rest.
